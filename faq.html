Frequently asked questions¶

Below you find a list for known errors, that may occur and how to work around:

1. HDF5surrogate escape NameError: global name ‘exc’ is not defined

export LC_ALL=en_AU.utf8 to your .bashrc

2. Theano MKL support RuntimeError: To use MKL 2018 with Theano you MUST set “MKL_THREADING_LAYER=GNU” in your environement. export MKL_THREADING_LAYER=GNU to your .bashrc
